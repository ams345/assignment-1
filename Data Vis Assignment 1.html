Dataset: Fatal Accidental Overdoses

Overall Analysis Questions

  1. What are the trends in fatal overdoses over time?
  2. What substances are most commonly associated with fatal overdoses?

Discoveries & Insights

Summary

The set of visualizations provides an overview of overdose deaths over time, revealing the increasing severity of the drug crisis in recent years. The gender disparity in overdose deaths is made apparent with the visualizations, with males adversely and disproportionately affected compared to females. Opioids, especially fentanyl, have become the main substances involved in overdose fatalities, superseding heroin and other prescription opioids. Furthermore, demographic patterns suggest potential environmental or social factors influencing overdose rates, such as age, while the racial breakdown of overdose deaths indicates disparities in how different racial groups are impacted by substance abuse. To be useful in further understanding the drug epidemic and developing responsive measures, more data needs to be collected and analysis needs to be completed.
